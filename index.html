About Us

Building Infrastructure,
One Pipe at a Time

Buildsurge Irrigation Private Limited is a Private Limited Company incorporated on 8 September 2022, registered under ROC Pune. With our head office in Balaji Nagar, Pune (411046) and our manufacturing plant in Jejuri, Maharashtra, we specialize in producing high-quality HDPE pipes.

Our pipes range from 2 inch to 8 inch diameter, serving diverse sectors including agriculture, irrigation, road construction, and utility water distribution. We are committed to delivering durable, reliable piping solutions that power India's infrastructure growth.
